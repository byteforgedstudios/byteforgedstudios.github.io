Request account & data deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your account and any data associated with it for apps by ByteForged Studios.

If your email client doesn't open automatically, please copy the template (right) and send it to ByteForgedStudios@gmail.com.

What happens next

We will verify your identity and the account details you provide. For security we might request additional information (for example a screenshot showing the account email in-app, or the device ID). Deletion requests are processed within 30 days unless otherwise required by local law.

Privacy note: We will only use the information you submit to identify and delete the account and associated data. We will not share your personal details with third parties except where required by law.
